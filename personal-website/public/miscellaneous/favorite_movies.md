# My Favorite Movies
I have previously written about my favorite books, so I thought I'd do the same for movies and TV-shows. When I did it for books I wrote a paragraph about each book. This time I will only write a sentence or two about each movie and TV-show. 

## Movies
- **Manchester by the Sea** One of the saddest movies I have watched and shows how fast life can go from perfect to terrible. 
- **A Star is Born** A depressingly realistic story about how we tend to make the same mistakes many times even though we know they are mistakes.The music is great.
- **The Greatest Showman** Great music and plot. I have a soft spot for good music.
- **Shutter Island** The movie is good, but the ending is superb.
- **Elskling** (Loveable) One of the few good Norwegian movies. It portrays the dynamics of a relationship very well and it encourages reflection about self.
- **Shawshank Redemption** Everyone loves this and it is deserved.
- **Good Will Hunting** Good movies often have one or two scenes you remember, Good Will Hunting has at least six. The dialogue is exceptional.
- **A Man Called Ove** (the Swedish version) A reminder that we are all humans and some people who appear cold and cynical might have the biggest heart.
- **The Intouchables** (the French version) A great story about an unlikely friendship. "O Captain! My Captain!"
- **Dead Poets Society** Do everything with love and *live deliberately*.
- **Me Before You** Another story of how fast life can change for the worse and a reminder that we should all appreciate and be grateful for what we have.

## TV-shows
- **Breaking Bad** Is simply one of the best shows ever created.
- **Game of Thrones** Great until the last season. I have read some of the books and they are also great. 
- **Better Call Saul** I always say that shows that move slowly and use a lot of time building the story are the greatest. This is one of them. The ending is also great! Contains some of the best dialogues ever written. 
- **Lilyhammer** Maybe the best Norwegian TV-shows ever created. A good dose of action in addition to being very funny.
- **Avatar: The Last Airbender** one of the coolest animated shows.
- **Prison Break** One of the first TV-shows I watched and I keep coming back to it.
- **After Life** A masterpiece in combining sadness, comedy, and compassion.
- **Blacklist** Interesting story.
