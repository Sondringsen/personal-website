# Favorite Books, Movies and TV Shows
I used to read a lot when I was younger but stopped sometime during high school as I developed other interests. As many teenagers do, I shifted towards watching more TV-shows and movies. For me, watching movies was and still is something I usually do with friends and family while I enjoy TV-shows alone. 

When I started university, I began reading again as I started reminiscing about all the good experiences I had with books as a child. My reading is usually something that is a bit on-and-off; in some periods I read a lot and in other periods I almost don’t read at all. For some time, I read a lot of popular science as I wanted to learn in a relaxed setting. Lately, I have found myself picking up the classics as they explore profound things in the human psyche or society which I find very interesting. A book’s ability to do this convincingly is usually what makes me like a book or not.

Many times, I just watch movies and TV-shows for their entertainment purposes, but this is rarely what makes me like a movie. Similarly to books, although books usually do it better, movies and TV shows can reveal deep insights about humans and society and is often a deciding factor in why I like them. Another very important thing for me is the writing or the storytelling and sometimes also the music. 

Below I have made a list in no particular order of my favorite books, movies and TV-shows and I hope it can inspire the choice of your next read or watch!
