# Reading List :books:

I used to read a lot when I was younger but as school and work took more time I didn't have the mental capacity nor the motivation to read as much. However, I still try to read whenever I am motivated and whenever I don't have too much work on my plate. Below are lists of fiction and non-fiction books I want to read. I put together the lists with the help of my good friend Mr. Claude (thank you Anthropic) based on my interests and similarity to books I tend to like. As always I want to maintain a nonzero $\epsilon$ in my life, so there are some random choices in there for exploration purposes. The lists are in no particular order. I will try to write a review on each of them as I work my way through the list.
